Overview

Sobha Royal Pavillion

SOBHA presents the latest upcoming mixed residential apartment project in the in-demand neighborhood of Hadosiddapura, Sarjapur Road, Bangalore – Sobha Royal Pavilion. This residential apartment project in East Bangalore features the very best in Sobha’s signature luxury, safety, and beautiful landscapes all around thus providing a comfortable living environment for adults and children alike.

Sobha Royal Pavilion is guaranteed to bring a quality living experience to the community of Sarjapur Road with brilliant architecture and equivalent lifestyle. SOBHA, the developer, is one of India’s leading real estate developers having produced some of the Bangalore’s and the country’s most recognizable properties over the past few decades. Sobha Royal Pavilion is located right on Sarjapur Road near Bellandur and Whitefield housing 2, 3 and 4 BHK spacious Apartments with luxurious exteriors and interiors.

Amenities at Sobha Royal Pavilion include a fully equipped clubhouse, health centers, housekeeping and assisted care facilities, gymnasium, swimming pool, recreation rooms, outdoor sports courts, children’s play area, party hall, and much more. With meticulous planning and utmost importance to state-of-the-art 24/7 security, the upcoming Sobha Royal Pavilion is your dream home in Bangalore.
